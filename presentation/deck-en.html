06 · Demo · Privacy audit

Privacy Score — a real number, not marketing.

The APK scanner computes a risk score for every installed app from 5 signals:

  • Accessibility Service usage (potential keylogger)
  • Device Admin claim
  • Debug certificate (unsigned app)
  • Outdated targetSdk (bypasses new protections)
  • Suspicious installer (unknown sideload source)

Plus a device-wide Privacy Score from VPN / Private DNS / Wi-Fi security / IPv6 signals.
