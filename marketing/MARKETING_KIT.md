catalogs, and community/launch sites. English **and** Polish. Positioning is **free-first**:
lead with the free, open-source core; the optional Pro key is mentioned only lightly.

> **Honesty guardrails — keep every claim true (avoids PUP / "misleading" rejections):**
> The app has **opt-in** ads + affiliate links and an **optional** paid Pro key. So:
> - ✅ Say: *no telemetry · no bundleware/PUP · no forced ads · no nag screens · core is free & open-source (MIT)*
> - ✅ Ads/affiliate are **off by default** and **opt-in** — you may say that, or simply omit it.
> - ❌ Never say "no ads ever," "no premium tier," or "100% free, nothing paid."
> - For the **strictest** portals (Softpedia, FossHub, PortableApps), use the variants marked **[STRICT]** that omit monetization entirely while staying truthful.
